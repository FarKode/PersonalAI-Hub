Built on Privacy and Customization

Traditional AI apps collect your chat history, sell metrics, and lock you into monthly subscriptions. PersonalAI Hub does the opposite.

🔒 On-Device Encryption

Your API keys are encrypted using Android's native Keystore system and stored locally on your device. They are sent directly to the AI provider over HTTPS, never passing through any server controlled by us.

🚀 Bring Your Own Key (BYOK)

Use your existing developer keys for Groq (FREE tier), Google Gemini (FREE tier), OpenAI, OpenRouter, or Anthropic. Pay only for what you query directly to the providers, avoiding $20/month subscription traps.

💾 High-Performance Database

Using a native local ObjectBox database, all your document chunks, search vectors, and message histories remain safely on your smartphone storage. 100% private, 100% yours.
