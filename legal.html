Privacy Policy & Terms

Last updated: July 2026  ·  Applies to: PersonalAI Hub (Android App)

🔒 Core Principle: PersonalAI Hub is built on a Zero Data Collection foundation. We have no backend server. We collect nothing. Your data never leaves your device.

1️⃣ Our Core Philosophy: Zero Data Collection

At PersonalAI Hub, your privacy is not a feature — it is the entire foundation of the app. We operate on a strict Zero Data Collection policy.

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
  • We do not run analytics, usage tracking, or telemetry of any kind.
  • We do not have a backend server. There is no server to send your data to.

2️⃣ How Your Documents Are Processed

When you upload a document (PDF, TXT) to the Document Mind agent:

  • Text extraction, chunking, and vector embedding are computed entirely on your device.
  • All document data and vector embeddings are stored in a local ObjectBox database on your phone.
  • Your documents never leave your device. They are never uploaded to any cloud storage.

3️⃣ API Key Security (BYOK Model)

PersonalAI Hub uses a Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) architecture:

  • You provide your own API key (OpenAI, Gemini, Groq, Anthropic, OpenRouter, etc.).
  • Your API key is encrypted using Android's native Keystore system via flutter_secure_storage. It is never stored in plain text.
  • When you send a prompt, the app communicates directly from your device to the AI provider's servers (e.g., api.openai.com). Your key never passes through any server we operate.
✅ AES Encrypted on Device ✅ Android Keystore Backed ✅ No Intermediate Server ✅ Direct HTTPS to Provider

4️⃣ Third-Party AI Providers

When you send a prompt or document chunk to an AI provider via your API key, that specific text is subject to the respective provider's own privacy policy (e.g., OpenAI's Data Usage Policy, Google's Gemini API Terms). We encourage you to review their policies.

5️⃣ Chat History & Local Data

All chat history, conversation logs, and agent preferences are stored exclusively in the local ObjectBox database on your device. They are never synced to any cloud service. You can delete all local data at any time by uninstalling the app.

6️⃣ Terms of Use

PersonalAI Hub is provided "as is." By using the app, you agree to the following:

  • You are solely responsible for managing your API keys and any associated costs charged by your chosen AI provider (OpenAI, Groq, Gemini, etc.).
  • The app does not guarantee the accuracy, reliability, or legality of AI-generated responses.
  • Do not use the app to process confidential or legally protected documents if your AI provider's terms prohibit it.
  • Any lifetime Pro purchase (if applicable) unlocks local app features only and does not cover third-party API costs.

7️⃣ Open Source & Transparency

The source code for PersonalAI Hub is publicly available on GitHub. You are welcome to audit the code to verify our privacy claims. We believe transparency is the strongest privacy guarantee.

8️⃣ Changes to This Policy

If we ever update our privacy practices, we will update this page with a new "Last updated" date and notify users within the app update release notes.
